Apply to Boston University


The CAS On-Campus Internship Program is an excellent way to gain
internship experience right here at BU!

Before applying to positions at the OECD please check that you meet
the following criteria:

  • You must be a domestic or international student.
  • You are less than 35 years of age.
  • You are fluent in English.
  • Must possess strong academic background
  • Internship commitment is approximately 6 hours per week
    for the full semester.

Before beginning this application form, be sure to review the
information here and our FAQ page in regards to eligibility requirements,
associated 1-credit course, and program expectations.
