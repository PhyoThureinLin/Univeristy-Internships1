Life at Boston University

Your adventures await you!


The BU offers a wide variety of activities to promote staff health and wellbeing, including pilates, osteopathy and reflexology. Staff may also consult with social advisers and onsite medical personnel. Staff can benefit from the onsite gym and fitness facilities allowing easy access to affordable exercise and fitness classes.

The transition from high school to college brings great freedom of choice as you decide who you are and who you want to become. As your academic home, Boston University is committed to supporting your adventure in self-discovery by strongly emphasizing mentorship, community, and hands-on learning to help you identify and achieve your academic and personal goals.
