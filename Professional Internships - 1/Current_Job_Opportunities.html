Current Job Opportunities

Princeton University remains steadfast in our commitment to our teaching and research mission, especially during these challenging times. We currently are hiring in key positions that will enable us to fulfill our mission and advance our strategic priorities.

To view all positions currently open for application, see the Princeton University Careers site at hr.princeton.edu/careers.

Technical Support Specalist

In this role, you you provide support, technical guidance, and incident response for the University community. You also escalate issues when needed and work to raise the technical abilities of campus user through knowledge articles. (#2022-15912)

The job is ideal for someone who:

  • Enjoys troubleshooting issues and providing clear solutions
  • Thrives in a fast-paced working environme
  • Is interested in providing superior customer service in a multi-tiered support environment

Skills you bring to the table:

  • Working knowledge of various technologies including networking, Active Directory, email, and cloud-based solutions
  • Strong communication skills to provide customer support by phone, email, and chat

Research Data Storage Analyst

In this role, you partner with colleagues across campus to design, install, and administer data storage systems to meet the needs of data intensive research. You will also monitor and troubleshoot system-level issues and manage network configurations. (#2022-15881)

The job is ideal for someone who:

  • Enjoys finding creative solutions to routine and complex data storage and movement problems
  • Provides prompt and professional customer service

Skills you bring to the table:

  • Experience as a storage system administrator
  • Experience supporting various storage solutions, including cloud object storage systems and NAS

Information Security Operations Engineer

In this role, you are responsible for the day-to-day monitoring and maintenance of the information security tools utilized by the University’s Information Security Office (ISO). You also identify opportunities for continuous process improvement, recommend proactive solutions, and endorse best practices. (#2022-15863)

The job is ideal for someone who:

  • Enjoys interpreting security alerts, and analyzing exploit trends and threat feeds
  • Is interested in leveraging SIEM solutions for detection and management of security incidents, including fine-tuning and configuration of reporting, dashboards, and alerts
  • Is looking to train colleagues to understand security concepts and threat monitoring

Skills you bring to the table:

  • Strong experience in security and network monitoring and reporting tool evaluation/deployment
  • Experience using SIEM solutions, specifically LogRhythm
  • Strong experience in Incident response management and procedures
  • Solid understanding of IDS/IPS systems configuration and management

Senior PeopleSoft Developer/Analyst II

In this role, you serve as the PeopleSoft Human Capital Management (HCM) module subject matter expert. You analyze campus customer needs and develop customizations and bolt-on solutions. Your responsibilities also include PeopleSoft testing, maintenance, and customer support. (#2022-15586)

The job is ideal for someone who:

  • Is successful in analyzing customer needs and developing custom solutions, interfaces, and reports
  • Enjoys researching new technology features and making recommendations on adoption to enhance current processes

Skills you bring to the table:

  • Strong experience as a developer/analyst supporting PeopleSoft HCM
  • In-depth knowledge of PeopleSoft architecture and experience developing in PeopleTools utilizing Application Designer, Application Engine, PeopleCode, and Application Packages
  • Experience working with HTML5, CSS, XML, and Java Script
